# SignalSync AI

Convert business signals into sales opportunities in real time.

## Problem

Sales teams manually monitor hiring trends, funding rounds and business updates, causing valuable opportunities to be missed.

## Solution

SignalSync AI automatically:

- Detects business signals
- Analyzes buying intent
- Scores opportunities
- Generates outreach recommendations

## Features

- AI Pipeline
- Signal Feed
- Lead Board
- AI Agent Activity
- Operations Timeline

## Workflow

Business Signal → Intent Analysis → Lead Score → Opportunity → Outreach
